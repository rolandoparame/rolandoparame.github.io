About

I’ve developed a strong and widely transferable skill set primarily during my time as a store manager for Vodafone. By showing my ability with Excel and Video Creation I established a strong personal brand within my role and was nominated to create a sales tracker aligned to a new commission model on a national level. This project requires maintenance when there are pricing changes in the business, which is quite often, and to support other managers I’ve created video tutorials instructing how to update spreadsheets to help support my peers.

I’m confident in my problem-solving ability and can communicate effectively with people from a wide variety of cultural backgrounds. I believe my best quality is my ability to work pragmatically when faced with a challenge. Additionally, I’m a structured, organised team leader and proficient across the Microsoft Suite with an affinity to Microsoft Excel. Throughout my working career, I’ve contributed to creating productive environments and state-wide team achievements through critical thinking and collaborative effort.

In addition to the above, I’m confident in my learning ability to obtain the specific finer skills to be efficient within this role. I hope to discuss the possibility of joining the team so please feel free to contact me for any additional details or to meet for an interview.

Thanks for taking the time to visit my website
